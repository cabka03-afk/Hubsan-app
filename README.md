## 📸 Extract invoice data → Sync to QuickBooks in seconds

A powerful, client-side web app that uses **OCR** to automatically extract customer names, invoice numbers, dates, items, and amounts from invoice images — then syncs directly to QuickBooks or other third-party apps.

![Hubsan Demo](https://via.placeholder.com/800x400?text=Invoice+OCR+Demo)

---

## ✨ Features

- ✅ **Customer Management** – Upload CSV/JSON lists or add individually
- ✅ **Product Inventory** – Track items with optional pricing
- ✅ **Smart Invoice Capture** – Upload image or take photo with camera
- ✅ **OCR Auto-Extraction** – Uses Tesseract.js to read invoice text
- ✅ **Editable Fields** – Review & correct extracted data
- ✅ **QuickBooks Sync** – One-click integration (mock + real API ready)
- ✅ **Third-Party Webhook** – Send to any external app (Zapier, custom API)
- ✅ **Local Persistence** – Data saves in browser's localStorage
- ✅ **Mobile Ready** – Responsive design with camera capture support
